Leadership & Board of Directors

The leadership of Nile Global Refinery Company Limited is composed of experienced professionals with extensive expertise in mining, energy, finance, and international business development. Our Board of Directors provides strategic oversight and governance, ensuring that the company operates in alignment with global standards of excellence, transparency, and accountability.

Chairman of the Board

Provides strategic leadership and governance oversight, guiding the company’s long-term vision, corporate direction, and stakeholder engagement at an international level.

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Responsible for overall operational management, execution of corporate strategy, and ensuring the company achieves its performance objectives across all sectors.

Chief Operations Officer (COO)

Oversees day-to-day operations including mining, refining, and logistics, ensuring efficiency, safety, and compliance with international standards.

Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

Manages financial strategy, risk management, and capital allocation, ensuring sustainable growth and strong financial performance.

Director of Exploration

Leads geological surveys, resource identification, and exploration programs, utilizing advanced technologies and data-driven methodologies.

Independent Non-Executive Director

Provides independent oversight, ensuring strong corporate governance, ethical practices, and balanced decision-making within the board.
