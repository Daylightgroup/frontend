Trade & Transaction Handling

Nile Global Refinery Company Limited ensures secure, transparent, and efficient trade and transaction handling across all operations. We adhere to international financial regulations and best practices to facilitate smooth global transactions for our partners and clients.

Transaction Process

1. Inquiry & Agreement

Clients initiate inquiries, followed by negotiation and agreement on terms, pricing, and delivery conditions.

2. Contract & Documentation

Formal contracts are issued, including compliance documentation, certificates, and legal agreements.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are executed through secure and internationally recognized financial channels.

4. Delivery & Logistics

Products are delivered under agreed terms with full documentation and tracking.

5. Final Settlement

Final verification, settlement, and completion of transaction with all compliance checks fulfilled.

Accepted Payment Methods

Bank Wire Transfer (SWIFT)

Secure international transfers through recognized banking institutions worldwide.

Letters of Credit (LC)

Widely accepted trade finance instrument ensuring secure transactions between buyers and sellers.

Documentary Collections

Payment collection via banks against shipping documents for secure trade settlements.

Escrow Services

Third-party escrow arrangements for high-value transactions ensuring trust and transparency.

Mobile Money

Regional mobile payment solutions for convenient and fast local transactions.

Credit / Debit Cards

Secure card payments via globally recognized payment gateways.

Digital Wallets

Payments via platforms such as PayPal and other secure e-wallet systems.

Cryptocurrency

Selected transactions may be conducted using major cryptocurrencies subject to compliance policies.

Security & Compliance

All transactions are conducted under strict compliance with international financial regulations, anti-money laundering (AML) policies, and know-your-customer (KYC) procedures. We prioritize transparency, traceability, and the highest levels of financial security.
